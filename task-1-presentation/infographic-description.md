## Task 1 Write-up

For Task 1, I created a "Digital Literacy Awareness" infographic to explain important digital skills in a simple and clear way. I used Canva to design the infographic because it is easy to use and provides good templates.

In my infographic, I covered key topics related to digital literacy. First, I explained what digital literacy means and why it is important. Then, I included safe internet practices like using strong passwords and avoiding unknown links. I also added email etiquette, such as writing proper subject lines and using formal language. Lastly, I mentioned useful digital tools that students can use in their daily academic work.

While making this infographic, the challenging part was to keep the content short but meaningful. I had to choose important points and present them clearly without making it too crowded. At the same time, I found it interesting to design the layout and arrange the content in a visually attractive way.

This task helped me understand how to present information in a simple and effective manner.
