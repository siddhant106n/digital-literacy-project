# Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist for College Students

## 8 Practical Safety Tips

1. Never share your UPI PIN or OTP  
Your UPI PIN and OTP are confidential. They are only required when making payments, not for receiving money.

2. Avoid scanning unknown QR codes  
Do not scan QR codes sent by unknown people, as they may lead to unauthorized transactions.

3. Verify job or internship offers  
Always confirm job or internship emails by checking the official company website before sharing any personal details.

4. Use strong passwords and enable 2FA  
Create strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication for better account security.

5. Be careful with suspicious links  
Avoid clicking on unknown links received through SMS, email, or WhatsApp messages.

6. Do not use public Wi-Fi for payments  
Avoid logging into banking apps or making payments while using public or unsecured Wi-Fi networks.

7. Protect your personal information  
Do not share sensitive details like Aadhaar number, bank details, or ID cards on social media.

8. Check your bank activity regularly  
Monitor your bank account and transaction history regularly to detect any suspicious activity early.
