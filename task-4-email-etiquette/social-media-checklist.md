# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

## Do’s for Responsible Social Media Use

1. Do check your privacy settings regularly  
Keep your accounts private and control who can see your posts and personal information.

2. Do think before posting  
Always remember that your posts can be seen by teachers, employers, or others in the future.

3. Do verify information before sharing  
Make sure news or content is true before sharing it to avoid spreading misinformation.

4. Do use social media for learning and networking  
Follow useful pages, connect with professionals, and use platforms like LinkedIn for growth.

5. Do take permission before posting others  
Always ask your friends or classmates before uploading their photos or videos.

6. Do follow positive and useful content  
Keep your feed clean by following educational and inspiring accounts.

7. Do take breaks from social media  
Spend less time online to maintain focus on studies and mental health.

---

## Don’ts for Responsible Social Media Use

1. Don’t share personal or sensitive information  
Avoid posting your phone number, address, ID cards, or bank details online.

2. Don’t engage in bullying or hate comments  
Respect others and avoid posting anything offensive or harmful.

3. Don’t post negative content about college or others  
Avoid complaining publicly about teachers, classmates, or college activities.

4. Don’t accept requests from unknown people  
Be careful with strangers as they may misuse your information.

5. Don’t use weak or same passwords everywhere  
Always use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

6. Don’t copy or repost content without credit  
Give proper credit if you use someone else’s work.

7. Don’t click on suspicious links  
Avoid unknown links, fake giveaways, or scams on social media.
